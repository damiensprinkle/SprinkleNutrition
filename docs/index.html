Privacy Policy

Soleus Fitness does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

All workout data is stored locally on your device using Apple's CoreData framework. No account is required. No data ever leaves your device. We have no access to your information.

About

Soleus Fitness is a clean, no-nonsense workout tracker built for people who just want to train. No subscriptions. No cloud accounts. No ads.
